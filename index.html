Something is looming in the Boiling Isles...

Take charge as a member of the Emperor's Coven to investigate mysterious disappearances. Your adventure will lead you around the Titan meeting a host of new characters and locations in this alternate universe Owl House game. Scroll down to learn more.

Visual Novel Meets Adventure

Converse with a cast of all new characters, and some familiar ones, with visual novel style dialog as the story unfolds. When the conversation ends, you'll explore all over the Boiling Isles going on quests, deciphering mysteries, and solving puzzles.

We are designing a story that we are confident compliments the world and characters of the Owl House while providing something new, original, and exciting. A fun collaboration between several talented hobby writers and artists.

Interested in joining us? Check this link here.

Our project is powered by the Unity Game Engine and a customized version of Naninovel.

This project is completely free and non-profit. This project has no relation to Disney nor any official member of the Owl House team.
